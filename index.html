Bio

I'm a master student in the Nanyang Tech S-Lab/NLP Lab. I'm interested in developing machine learning tools for information retrieval and natural language generation. My current research is focused on multi-modal information retrieval for video grounding.

Previously I was a research intern at the University of Notre Dame MINE and KAUST ITL.

I received my Bachelor degree in Computer Science and Technology from UESTC, from the UESTC honor class.

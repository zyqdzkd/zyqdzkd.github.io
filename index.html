Bio

I'm a master student in the Nanyang Tech S-Lab/NLP Lab advised by Aixin Sun. I'm interested in developing machine learning tools for information retrieval and natural language generation. My current research is focused on multi-modal information retrieval for video grounding.

I received my Bachelor degree in Computer Science and Technology from UESTC.
